--- layout: default --- Here's the process:

Identify

Identify areas for improvement through interviews, workshops and observation

Plan

Propose a tailored plan with clear and measurable goals for improvement

Deliver

Deliver appropriate in-house training to support the goals. Possible areas include:

  • Modern development processes based on agile and lean principles
  • Modern application architecture
  • Software technologies (including JavaScript and NoSQL databases)
  • Modern development techniques (including test-driven development, pair programming and Git workflows)
  • Continuous integration and continuous delivery

Support

We will then work with your teams supporting them in applying these new skills and technologies

Analyse

Reporting on the original goals and, together with the teams, recommending the next steps for further improvements
