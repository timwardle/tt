--- layout: default --- Here's the process Identify areas for improvement through interviews, workshops and observation Propose a tailored plan with clear and measurable goals for improvement Deliver appropriate in-house training to support the goals. Possible areas include: Modern development processes based on agile and lean principles Modern application architecture Software technologies (including JavaScript and NoSQL databases) Modern development techniques (including test-driven development, pair programming and Git workflows) Continuous integration and continuous delivery We will then work with your teams supporting them in applying these new skills and technologies Reporting on the original goals and, together with the teams, recommending the next steps for further improvements
